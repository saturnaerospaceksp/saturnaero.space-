ABOUT

A Strato-Launched Launch Vehicle

Hyperion is Saturn Aerospace's boldest launch concept so far: a strato-launched rocket carried aloft by Tarvos, our carrier aircraft, and released at around 20 to 30 kilometres above the ground.

By starting high above the densest part of the atmosphere, Hyperion can spend less propellant fighting drag and gravity losses during ascent. That means more of the vehicle's energy can be used to reach orbit and more of the stack can be devoted to payload.
