The People Behind Saturn

Saturn Aerospace is driven by a small team with different backgrounds, but the same goal: build better systems, fly cleaner missions, and keep pushing the program forward.

CEO

Caden

Caden joined the team and adapted quickly to the environment, showing a strong understanding of our systems and design approach. He started as a mission controller and quickly earned the CEO position.

COO

Aviation

Aviation has been with Saturn for many years and has shown steady growth throughout his time on the team. He now leads operations and contributes heavily to streaming and vehicle software.

CTO

Quasy founded the company in 2021, though he later reduced his role due to other commitments. He has created vehicle software, designed multiple launch vehicles, and pushed Saturn to its limits.
