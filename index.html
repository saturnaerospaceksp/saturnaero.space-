What is Saturn Aerospace?

Saturn Aerospace is a fictional space company that uses KSP to simulate rocket launches, programmed with a language called Kerboscript. The idea of Saturn Aerospace is to build colonies on other planets, such as the Moon & Mars.

Saturn to Saturn

Saturn Aerospace is planning to go further than near-Earth missions, with long-term goals focused on reaching Titan and expanding outward to build colonies across more worlds in the future.
