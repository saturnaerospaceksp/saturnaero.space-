About Us

What Is Saturn Aerospace?

Saturn Aerospace is a fictional space company built around Kerbal operations, with Kerboscript at the center of our flight tests, vehicle control, and launch-day reliability.

We operate a small fleet of launchers, including RHE4, Hyperion, and Daphnis, each designed to handle a different mission profile while staying focused on precision and repeatable outcomes.

Every vehicle is tuned with the same goal in mind: make the software do the hard work, keep the missions clean, and keep the launches moving forward.

Why Are We Different?

Why Are We Different?

We try to make rockets feel less mysterious. Our software handles a lot of the hard work, so the people running the mission can focus on getting it done cleanly.

  • We have a real mission control team. Launch operations are run by people who track the flight, make the calls, and keep the mission moving in the right direction.
  • Our team comes from around the world. Different backgrounds and perspectives help us solve problems faster and build better missions together.
  • We reuse as much as we can. Flight hardware and software are reused wherever possible to cut cost and shorten turnaround time.
  • We keep things simple on purpose. The goal is to make launches more reliable, easier to repeat, and less wasteful over time.
