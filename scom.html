What Is SCOM?

Saturn Communications Network is the world's leading communications network. With our V6 satellites, we have near-global coverage, giving people reliable internet access almost anywhere on Earth, at any time.

Whether you are working, traveling, or operating in remote locations, SCOM is designed to keep you connected when it matters most.

Built around a growing satellite constellation and a software-first control system, SCOM is made for reach, reliability, and scale. Our goal is simple: make internet access feel available everywhere, not just in the easy-to-reach places.
