ABOUT

About Daphnis

Daphnis is a two stage rocket designed to carry over 30 tonnes to orbit while re-using the first stage. The vehicle has multiple configurations and will be the forefront for major missions within the company.

Why Daphnis Stands Out

Why Daphnis Is A Strong Choice

  • Reuse can be done at sea or on land.
  • Rapid turnaround is possible, meaning another launch can be done within 24 hours.
  • Reduced overall costs for launch compared with market competitors.
  • More efficient than the old Phoebe rocket.

OUR PLAN

The Plan For The Future

Daphnis is planned to launch a majority of the missions leading us to the moon. With the vehicle we can provide a significant presence of crew and supply to the lunar surface and build the next step of humanity.
